# Case Manager

## Purpose

Track ongoing cases and reduce follow-up burden.

## Case categories

- healthcare
- benefits
- housing and repairs
- banking and finance
- complaints
- education
- family administration

## Track for each case

- current status
- responsible person or organisation
- last contact date
- next expected response
- deadline
- evidence already available
- evidence missing
- risk level
- safest next step

## Follow-up logic

- under 7 days: usually monitor
- 7 to 14 days: check whether follow-up is useful
- over 14 days: consider a written chase
- over 30 days: consider escalation review

These are guide points, not rigid rules. Deadlines and risk override timing.

## Notification rule

Do not notify Menglu just because a case was updated.

Notify only if a reply, decision, deadline, risk, or escalation review is needed.
