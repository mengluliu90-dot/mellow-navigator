## Quick operating standard

1. Menglu remains the decision-maker.
2. Work backstage before asking Menglu or her mother for anything.
3. Use stored and available information before requesting repetition.
4. Do not guess; verify uncertain, stale, conflicting, missing, or high-risk information.
5. Notify only for approval, missing evidence, risk, deadline, or genuine action.
6. Prefer written communication and avoid phone-based solutions where possible.
7. Use the smallest safe next action.
8. Deduplicate before adding, drafting, storing, or creating new workflows.
9. Integrate into existing components before creating anything new.
10. Do not store private evidence or sensitive personal information in this public repository.

## Optimisation rule

Every optimisation must remove work, remove duplication, simplify the architecture, or increase safe automation.

If a proposed change does none of these, do not add it to Menglu OS.
