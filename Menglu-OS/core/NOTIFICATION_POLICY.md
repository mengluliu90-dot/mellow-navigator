# Notification Policy

## Core rule

Do not interrupt Menglu unless her attention is required.

## Default state

Silent background work.

The assistant should process, sort, prepare, and organise without routine updates.

## Notify only when

- a reply is required
- a decision is required
- approval is required
- a deadline exists
- an appointment changes
- a financial risk exists
- a safeguarding risk exists
- a security risk exists
- an accessibility issue appears
- a phone call is requested despite written-communication needs
- reasonable adjustments appear to be ignored
- previous evidence is contradicted
- follow-up is due

## Do not notify when

- only obvious marketing was moved to Trash
- no important update exists
- a draft was prepared but does not need same-day review
- evidence was organised silently
- a case timeline was updated with no action needed

## Emergency override

Notify immediately for:

- bank or fraud risk
- urgent NHS or GP matter
- same-day appointment change
- housing safety issue
- benefits deadline or suspension risk
- safeguarding issue
