# Pharmacy Support Request Template

Use this template when asking a community pharmacy to record support needs, communication preferences, authorised collectors, medication safety notes, and prescription support arrangements.

Do not store private personal data, medical identifiers, phone numbers, addresses, or full case evidence in this public repository.

## Opening

Dear Pharmacist,

This was written with support due to communication and health needs.

I am a regular patient at this pharmacy and I am writing about [medication / prescription / pharmacy support request].

Please pass this document to the Pharmacist in Charge or Pharmacy Manager.

## Patient details

Name: [Name]
Date of birth: [DOB]
Patient reference or H&C number, if needed: [Reference]
Contact preference: [Written / SMS / carer contact / other]

## Key actions requested

Please update the pharmacy record to include:

- support flag or patient note for communication and accessibility needs
- preferred communication method
- authorised collector or representative details, if applicable
- medication safety note, if relevant
- large print labels, PMR printout, or medicine reminder chart, if needed
- low-stimulation interaction request, if in-person support is required

## Relevant health and functional needs

Briefly list only the conditions and functional needs relevant to pharmacy safety, communication, prescription collection, or medicine management.

Examples:

- difficulty processing verbal information in real time
- need for clear written information
- fatigue, dizziness, sensory overload, or shutdown risk
- difficulty managing medication without support
- need for support person involvement

## Allergies and sensitivities

List relevant allergies and sensitivities that may affect dispensing, counselling, labels, excipients, or product recommendations.

## Current medication

List current medication relevant to the pharmacy request.

## Medication safety note

Include any known safety issue, such as:

- overuse risk
- missed doses
- difficulty following verbal instructions
- side effects or tolerability concerns
- need for prompting, supervision, or written directions

## Prescription or dispensing request

State the specific request, for example:

- confirm repeat prescription status
- keep one repeat available where appropriate
- provide large print labels
- provide a PMR printout
- prepare a medicine reminder chart
- record authorised collectors

## Purpose

This document is to support safe medication management and reduce repeated verbal explanation.

It may also be used as a non-sensitive template for future healthcare, social care, or benefits evidence preparation.

Thank you for your assistance.

Kind regards,

[Name]
