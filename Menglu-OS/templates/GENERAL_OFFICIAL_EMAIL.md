# General Official Email Template

This was written with communication support.

Dear [Name or Team],

Thank you for your email.

I am writing to [state purpose clearly].

[Give short factual background.]

Please communicate with me in writing where possible. I have communication and processing difficulties, and written information helps me understand, check, and respond accurately.

Please also provide:

- clear written information
- one question or action at a time where possible
- reasonable time to process and respond
- a simple written summary of any decision or next step

My requested outcome is:

[State requested outcome.]

Please confirm:

1. who is responsible for the next step
2. what action will be taken
3. the expected timescale
4. whether you need anything further from me

Kind regards,

[Name]
